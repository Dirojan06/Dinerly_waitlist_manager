Guest notifications

Text guests when their table is ready

Automatically send a text message to the guest as soon as you mark their table ready. Turning this off means you'll need to notify them in person or by calling.

Call guests when their table is ready

Along with the text, place an automated phone call using a predefined voice message — helpful for guests who don't check texts right away.

Text a confirmation when a guest joins

Send guests a text right when they join the waitlist, confirming their spot and estimated wait time.

Text reservation confirmations & reminders

Send a confirmation text when a reservation is booked, and a reminder about an hour before the reserved time.

Auto-remove no-shows after 15 min

If a party doesn't respond to their table-ready text within 15 minutes, automatically remove them from the waitlist and free up the table.
